About the conference

The Matrix Conference is organized by the Matrix Foundation and its sponsors. Several tracks spread on several days to discuss topics ranging from how Matrix powers the public sector to how encryption works.

What is the Matrix Conference?

In summer 2022, the vibrant Matrix community scrambled together to organise the Matrix Community Summit. Hackers, volunteers and developers working on Matrix could join together at c-base to spend some quality time together and share their work.

The team behind the Community Summit and The Matrix.org Foundation joined forces to build on the success of the former, and extend it to audiences from the public and private sector.

Who attends?

We’re building on the success of the developer-centric Matrix Community Summit to reach new audiences in the public and private sector.

Several organisations from the public and private sectors that rely on Matrix have already shared their enthusiasm for the conference and will send their teams. Don’t miss your chance to miss prospective partners, customers, or the next company you will work for!

Why attend?

The Matrix Conference is meant to be the place where hackers, volunteers, vendors and consumer organisations meet, share their interests, and start working together.

The conference is THE place to learn, show, hack, hire and contribute to Matrix projects. Whether you’re an individual or an organisation, this is where the ecosystem blends in.
